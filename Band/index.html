About Peral Jam

Peral Jam is an American rock band founded in Seattle, Washington in 1990. The founding members are Jeff Ament being the bass guitarist, Stone Gossard being the rhythm guitarist, Mike McCready being the lead guitarist, Eddie Vedder being the lead vocalist and a guitarist, and formerly Dave Krusen being the first drummer. The band was formed after the dissolution of Gossard and Ament's previous bands Green River and Mother Love Bone. The band found mainstrean success with their debut album, Ten, in 1991. Pearl Jam's music focuses on the alternative rock genre, as well as grunge and hard rock.
